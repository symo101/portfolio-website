Fuel Extractor System — OCR Pipeline & WhatsApp Integration

End-to-end fuel and maintenance management system. Drivers submit fuel and maintenance receipts via WhatsApp; the system uses PaddleOCR to automatically extract structured data — fuel amount, cost, vendor, date — from receipt images and stores it in Supabase.

Production Systems · Fireside Group

Fireside PI-DB: Secure Staff Directory & Audit Compliance Portal

A full stack internal system for managing employee records, department assignments, and company vehicle allocations. Built with Next.js and Supabase, it replaced fragmented spreadsheets with a single source of truth. Every change to an employee's role, supervisor, or vehicle assignment is automatically logged with who made it, what changed, and when, creating a permanent audit trail with no extra manual work.

Impact

Gave HR and management a fully auditable record of every personnel change, supporting internal compliance requirements. Centralized previously scattered employee, supervision, and vehicle assignment records into one system, cutting administrative lookup time. Prevented invalid states, like assigning a company vehicle to an employee who's no longer active, through enforced data rules rather than manual checks.

Next.js TypeScript Supabase PostgreSQL Argon2 RBAC Jest
Production Systems · Fireside Group

Multi-Region Fleet Reporting & Driver Behaviour Automation

An automation pipeline built with n8n, Python, Playwright, FastAPI, Docker, and PostgreSQL/Supabase that runs fleet distance reporting and driver behaviour data ingestion across Nairobi, Mombasa, and combined HQ operations. It logs into the fleet tracking platform to generate and export motion reports, then monitors a shared mailbox for forwarded driver behaviour reports and parses Excel attachments across regions. Incoming records such as plates, alert types, timestamps, speed, region, and coordinates are cleaned, normalized, and upserted into the database on a schedule, with built in health checks and automated success and failure alerting.

Impact

Replaced a fully manual process of downloading and processing spreadsheets by hand across three regions. Standardized fleet telemetry and driver alert data into clean, database ready records, improving the reliability and traceability of daily operational reporting.

n8n Python Playwright FastAPI Docker PostgreSQL Supabase
Production Systems · Fireside Group

Vehicle Compliance Alert System: Insurance & Inspection Tracking

An automated compliance system monitoring insurance and inspection expiry dates across a 98-vehicle fleet. Running on a daily schedule, it cross-references vehicle records against expiry dates and sends WhatsApp alerts directly to the stakeholders responsible for fleet compliance, including logistics leadership and the drivers whose vehicles are affected, giving them advance warning before a vehicle's documents lapse.

Impact

Eliminated manual tracking of compliance deadlines across the entire fleet. Reduced the risk of regulatory non-compliance and unexpected vehicle downtime. Replaced reactive discovery of lapsed documents with proactive, automatic alerts to the people responsible for acting on them.

Node.js node-cron Google Sheets API Evolution API (WhatsApp) Automation
